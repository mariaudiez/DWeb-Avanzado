Program & Activities

In THALÍA, every brushstroke opens a universe.

For three days, the Crystal Palace of Madrid becomes a cathedral of light, hosting a journey through painting, dialogue, and presence.

Each moment of the program invites the viewer to immerse themselves in the feminine gesture (contemplative, powerful, and alive.)

Day 1

Opening Ceremony - "Invocation to Light"

Time:10:00 - 11:00 AM

Location: Main Hall, Crystal Palace, Parque de el Retiro

Description: The festival opens with a symbolic lighting ceremony where the twenty participating artists collectively activate the space. Projected reflections dance across the glass dome while curator and director introduce the THALÍA concept and its mission to celebrate the feminine voice in painting.

Performance – “The Breath of the Canvas”

Time: 12:00 – 1:00 PM

Location: Central Pavilion

Description: A live, meditative performance blending movement, sound, and pigment. A dancer paints directly onto suspended fabrics, embodying the creative act as ritual — an homage to the muse Thalía and the living material of paint.

Welcome Cocktail & Private Viewing

Time: 7:00 – 9:00 PM

Location: Garden terrace surrounding the Crystal Palace

Description: An exclusive evening encounter among artists, collectors, and guests. Champagne and conversation flow while the sunset refracts through the glass, marking the beginning of THALÍA’s journey.

Day 2

Exhibition – “Twenty Voices on Canvas”

Time:10:00 AM – 8:00 PM

Location: Entire Crystal Palace

Description: The core of the festival. Twenty women artists from around the world present their works on canvas, each occupying an individual light-bathed section of the palace. The exhibition explores gesture, texture, and the feminine gaze in contemporary painting.

Guided Tour – “Reading the Gesture”

Time: 11:30 AM & 4:00 PM

Location: Start at North Entrance

Description: A curated walk led by the festival’s art mediators. Visitors experience the exhibition through reflections on material, color, and emotion, discovering how each artist translates presence into pigment.

Artist Talks – “Matter, Silence, and Emotion”

Time:5:30 – 7:00 PM

Location: South Wing, Lecture Area

An open dialogue with selected artists — Irene Pérez, Céline Westlund, and Marta Monzón Rosique — exploring process, intuition, and the balance between control and accident on canvas.

Day 3

Roundtable – “The Feminine Gaze in Contemporary Painting”

Time: 11:00 AM – 12:30 PM

Location: Main Hall

Description: A conversation between curators, critics, and artists discussing visibility, representation, and the role of women in contemporary fine arts. Moderated by cultural journalist Laura Santamaría.

Workshop – “From Light to Color”

Time: 2:00 – 4:00 PM

Location: Side Pavilion Workshop Area

Description: Interactive session led by Begoña Martín and Larissa Prediger. Participants explore experimental painting techniques using natural pigments and transparency, inspired by the glass architecture of the venue.

Closing Ceremony – “Echoes of Thalía”

Time: 7:00 – 9:00 PM

Location: Central Pavilion

Description: Final evening of THALÍA. A collective reflection where projected fragments of each artist’s work merge in light and sound, symbolizing unity through diversity. Includes live music and the presentation of the THALÍA Honor Award for artistic innovation.

Prices & Ticket levels

Experience THALÍA from within. Choose the form of access that reflects your own way of feeling and living contemporary art.

Ticket purchases are made directly at the entrance of the Crystal Palace, El Retiro Park — at the festival’s main ticket desk.

THALÍA believes in the beauty of presence: no online purchases, no rush — simply arrive, enter, and let the experience unfold.
